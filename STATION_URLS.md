## QR Codes

Each station should have a QR code printed and placed at the physical location. The QR code should point to the station's unique URL above.

**Instructions for customers:**
1. Scan the QR code at your station
2. Select rental duration
3. Enter phone number
4. Complete payment via Waafi
5. Collect your power bank

## Notes

- All stations use the same codebase
- Each station is automatically identified by its subdomain
- Payments are processed through Waafi Pay
- Power banks are managed via HeyCharge API for HeyCharge stations and the AppSphere bridge for AppSphere/Besiter stations
